Enter a phone number that you can receive SMS text messages at. You'll need to have access to this phone when logging in to your account.

Set Up Google Authenticator

  1. Install and open Google Authenticator on your mobile device.

    Search the App Store or Play store for "Google Authenticator".

  2. Add this account to Google Authenticator.
    • Tap the plus icon.
    • Scan this barcode.
    • Paste in this key:
    {{factor.secret}}
  3. Enter the code shown for {{factor.issuer}} in Google Authenticator.
